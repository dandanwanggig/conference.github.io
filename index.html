UC3M-SD Econometric Group

It is a great pleasure to invite you to the Workshop on Modelling Economic and Financial Time Series, hosted by the Statistics Department and Institute Flores de Lemus of the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (Campus Puerta de Toledo), Spain, on Friday June 7, 2018.

The UC3M-SD Econometric Group is a forum based at the Department of Statistics at the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid for the exchange and dissemination of research work in econometrics, time series and their applications in the fields of economics and finance. The workshop provides an excellent opportunity for networking among researchers and academics and high quality feedback.

If you would like to attend this event please register through the link. We welcome you to present in our post session.Please submit abstracts for the workshop under the theme “Modelling Economic and Financial Time Series” through abstract submission.
